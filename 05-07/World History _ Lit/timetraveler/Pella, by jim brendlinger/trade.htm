PELLA

Pella had a very big port

From this port came from merchants with fine goods willing to trade with Macedonia

This made Macedonia a place where many cultures would meet

The point were the city would buy stuffs from itself was the agora, where the streets from the port lead to

The agora was the city center, which habitated lots of shops for trade and many people
